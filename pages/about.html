Hi! I’m Dhyani
Nice to meet you.

I’m a 23 year old product designer studying at the National Institute of Design, Ahemdabad. Gujarati by birth, I was born and brought up in Mumbai. I believe design research is best done around some plants and breaks best taken with some tea. While I try to attempt and articulate my feelings into rhyming lines, I love to run 5Ks. And when I’m not making mind maps, I would probably be making puns or cakes.
